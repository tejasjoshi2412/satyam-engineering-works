Compliance

Built to NBC-aligned standards

Fire fighting systems are designed and installed with reference to the National Building Code (NBC) fire safety provisions applicable to the occupancy type — factory, warehouse, or commercial building. Pipe sizing, hydrant spacing, and sprinkler coverage are worked out against the site's layout and load before fabrication begins.

Every system is pressure-tested before handover, with documentation provided on request for statutory fire NOC and insurance purposes.
