Process Flow

Drawing to erected structure

01
Site Survey & Design Input
We review drawings or survey the site to confirm load, span, and layout requirements.
02
Fabrication
Structural members are cut, drilled, and welded in-house to drawing specification.
03
Site Erection
Fabricated members are transported and erected on-site by our own crew.
04
Welding & Alignment Check
Structural joints are welded and checked for alignment before proceeding.
05
Painting & Handover
Anti-corrosive priming and finish coating are applied before documented handover.
