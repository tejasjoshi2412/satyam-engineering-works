Satyam Engineering Works is a Bhavnagar-based engineering contractor working across three disciplines — fire protection, investment casting finishing, and structural steel fabrication.

Our Story

Built on the yard, not the brochure

Satyam Engineering Works started as a small fabrication outfit in Bhavnagar and grew alongside the industrial belt around it — Chitra GIDC, the port-linked manufacturing units, and the foundries that supply them. Over 15+ years, the same instinct has held: keep fabrication and execution in-house, keep crews direct, and take on the work other contractors subcontract away.

That approach shaped three connected divisions rather than one general contracting business — fire line erection for factories and warehouses, casting finishing work for the foundries nearby, and structural steel fabrication for the sheds and platforms that house them all. Our Approach

Why clients stay with us

Four principles that run through every division.

  • 01
    Own FabricationFittings, structural members, and finishing work are handled in-house — not outsourced — so quality is controlled end to end.
  • 02
    Direct Crew, No MiddlemenErection and execution are carried out by our own welders and fitters, not subcontracted teams.
  • 03
    Built for ComplianceWork is carried out to relevant engineering, fire safety, and NBC standards depending on the scope of the job.
  • 04
    Repeat Industrial ClientsFactories, foundries, and builders across Gujarat return to us for expansions, finishing work, and AMC renewals.
