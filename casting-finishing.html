Our Services

Full finishing line, in-house

Eleven finishing disciplines under one roof — no batch leaves for outside processing.

Fettling

Removal of flash, gates, and risers to bring castings to finished dimension.

Swing Grinding

Heavy-duty swing frame grinding for large gates and riser removal.

Belt Grinding

Continuous belt grinding for even surface stock removal.

Die Grinding

Precision die grinder work for tight radii, cavities, and detail areas.

Buffing

Rotary buffing for a smooth, uniform surface finish on cast components.

Mirror Polishing

Final-stage high-gloss polishing for showcase or precision components.

Shot Blasting

Shot blast cleaning to strip scale, rust, and sand residue from castings.

Sand Blasting

Abrasive blasting for heavy-duty surface prep on large or oxidised castings.

Glass Bead Blasting

Fine bead blasting for a soft, satin surface finish without pitting.

Weld Repair

Repair welding of casting defects — porosity, cracks, and cold shuts.

Inspection

Dimensional and visual inspection before dispatch, per client specification.

Process Flow

Receiving to dispatch

The same sequence for every batch, scaled to order size.

01
Receiving & Inspection
Incoming castings are checked against the order specification before processing begins.
02
Fettling
Flash, gates, and risers are removed to bring the casting to finished dimension.
03
Grinding
Swing, belt, and die grinding even out the surface and remove tool marks.
04
Blasting
Shot, sand, or glass bead blasting cleans scale and prepares the surface finish.
05
Polishing
Buffing and mirror polishing bring components to the required surface finish.
06
Final Inspection & Dispatch
Dimensional and visual checks are completed before the batch is packed and dispatched.
